Inception

The Social Programmers Union

The Goal of the SPU is to maintain a social front for technologists to discuss and act on currently outstanding issues. An issue can be related to any civic/environmental/judicial or business related topic without prejudice. The SPU is intended to be a consortium and is not to be held liable for the actions of any or all of it's members based on proceedings held on this site or at designated SPU fronts or activities.

Becoming a Member

In order to become a member, a user must submit a script, Jupyter Notebook or module for incorporation into the SPU social framework or own and operate a social web site. The framework is a series of exercies, workbooks and modules that demonstrate issues that have been verified by voting members of the union as important issues. In adddition to the social framework, election software will be provisioned so that SPU fronts can contribute votes from their site on proposed social issues. Verified members of the SPU can apply to administer election relays.
